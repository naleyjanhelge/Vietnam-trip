# Project Specification: "Operation Vietnam" Landing Page

**Target Audience:** A group of male friends ("Gutta") planning a trip in March 2026.

**Goal:** Persuade the group to choose **Vietnam** over Thailand.

**Tone:** High energy, luxurious but budget-smart, adventurous, "Amsterdam vibes," and exclusive.

**File Structure:** All images listed below are located in the **root directory** (`./`). `index.html` and `styles.css` (or scripts) should also be in the root.

----------

## 1. Core Pitch & Narrative (Source Material)

_Data extracted from project planning documents._

### The Main Argument: Vietnam vs. Thailand

-   **The Vibe:** Thailand (Phuket) feels touristy with a "tourist tax" on everything. Vietnam offers a more authentic feel with "Amsterdam vibes" in the city and hidden beaches on the islands.
    
-   **Traffic & Freedom:** In Vietnam (Phu Quoc), traffic is lighter. We can rent scooters/cars and drive ourselves to find hidden spots. In Phuket, distances are huge and transport is expensive.
    
-   **The "King of Budget":** Vietnam is described as the "King of low budget without feeling cheap". We get luxury for a fraction of the price.
    
-   **Activities:** We get unique experiences like "Grand World" (Venice in Vietnam), world-record cable cars, and rooftop gaming bars.

## 3. Technical Implementation Details

-   **Responsiveness:** Mobile-first design (the boys will likely view this on phones).
    
-   **Animations:**
    
    -   Fade-in elements on scroll.
        
    -   Parallax effect on the Cable Car images.
